Today, my podcast launched our Patreon. I had hoped to finish the write of the 2018 Listener Survey analysis I started, but as it turns out, I had a lot of other projects to write up.

Most of them probation related.

BUT. In honor of the launch and to get back into the swing of things, I present the word cloud from the ACC 2018 Listener Survey. The image below represent the reasons why people listen to our little show, featuring the tidytext package from the amazing Dr. Julia Silge.

a2_wordcloud <- a2  %>% unnest_tokens(word, answers) %>% anti_join(stop_words) %>% 
  count(word) %>% filter(n > 2) %>% 
  with(wordcloud(word, n, colors = spectral, random.order = FALSE, random.color = FALSE))
